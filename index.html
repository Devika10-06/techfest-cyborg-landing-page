// ABOUT

The Techfest Legacy

Techfest is the annual Science & Technology Festival of IIT Bombay — the premier institution of technological education and research in India.

Now in its 30th edition, Techfest has grown into Asia's largest tech festival, drawing innovators, engineers, scientists, and visionaries from across the globe. With over 1,80,000+ footfall and 300+ events spanning robotics, AI, space technology, and more — it is the definitive convergence of human ingenuity and machine intelligence.

  • International participants from 150+ countries
  • Nobel Laureates & Global Industry Leaders
  • Prize pool worth ₹1 Crore+
  • Live demonstrations & exhibits
