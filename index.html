The Techfest Legacy

Techfest is the annual Science & Technology Festival of IIT Bombay — India's premier institution of technological education.

Now in its 30th edition, Techfest stands as Asia's largest technology festival. Drawing innovators, engineers, scientists, and visionaries across the globe, it is the definitive convergence of human ingenuity and machine intelligence.

  • Participants from 150+ countries
  • Nobel Laureates & Industry Leaders
  • Prize pool worth ₹1 Crore+
